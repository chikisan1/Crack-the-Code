Testcase String Compression 

1. One character 
Input: "a"

2. 2 same characters 
Input: "aa"

3. 2 different characters 
Input: "ab"

4. n same characters (ex: 3)
Input: "aaa"
Output: a3

5. 3 different characters 
Input: "abc"
Output: abc

6. 2 same 1 different 
Input: "aab"
Output: aab

7. 2 same 2 different
Input: "aabb"
